Get Started

Congratulations on your new Track 44 email! You are allowed to do whatever you want with it. This is a private server hosted from QuickPacket, running Ubuntu Server 14.04 and Mail-In-A-Box. It is secure, locally hosted, and private.

What can you do?

You can use your browser to access the web client here, or you can add it to almost any mail application or app, but you will need a few settings

Common Mail Settings

Protocol/Method: IMAP

IMAP Port: 993

IMAP Security: TLS, otherwise SSL

SMTP Port: 587

SMTP Security: STARTTLS (always)

Username: ALWAYS username@track44.moe

Password: Email password

"My Mail Isn't Showing up in Inboxes

It will take some time and use for the larger email providers like Gmail and Yahoo to "trust" @track44.moe.

The more we use it, the quicker this happens.

Tell your point of contact to check their Spam/Junk folder if they haven't recieved your email.

Requesting Changes

If you want to change your @ there's a few options

- You give up your existing one and we give you the new one, you can't keep your old stuff

- You keep your old one and new one and can log in as both

- We create an "alias" that you can send/recieve as but you login with your old one

- We create your desired @ address and turn your old address into an "alias" you can send/recieve from. You will lose old emails.

"I forgot my password"
